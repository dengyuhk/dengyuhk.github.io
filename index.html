Short Biography

Deng Yu is now a fifth-year Ph.D. student at the School of Creative Media, City University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Hongbo Fu and Prof. Manfred Lau (co-supervisor). He is expected to graduate from CityU in October 2023. During his Ph.D. study, he has visited Dr. Youyi Zheng at State Key Lab of CAD & CG, Zhejiang University from 2019 to 2020 and Dr. Lin Gao at Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences during spring 2022.

His current research interests lie primarily in Computer Graphics, Sketch Understanding, Sketch-based Image Synthesis and 3D Modeling.
